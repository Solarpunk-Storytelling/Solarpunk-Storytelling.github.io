About the Prizes

The winning stories from the 2021 Showcase will be published in Solarpunk Magazine, Solarpunk Society Magazine, Shoreline of Infinity Science-Fiction Magazine, by the Rapid Transition Alliance, and in Extinction Rebellion publications. All winning stories were illustrated by a team of artists spread across the globe. The artwork has been published on this website.

All our adult and teenage winners were sent a copy of Sunvault: Stories of Solarpunk and Eco-Speculation, which we sourced from circular economy retailer Awesome Books.
